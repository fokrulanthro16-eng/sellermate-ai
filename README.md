\# SellerMate AI



AI-powered Seller Central for Bangladeshi e-commerce sellers.



SellerMate helps small e-commerce businesses manage:



\- Orders

\- Products

\- Inventory

\- Customers

\- Analytics

\- Bangla/English seller workflows

\- AI-powered business action suggestions



\## Current Status



This is an early private build.



Core modules included:



\- FastAPI backend

\- Next.js frontend

\- Product management

\- Inventory management

\- Order management

\- Customer management

\- Analytics

\- Strategic AI agent layer



\## Tech Stack



\- Backend: Python, FastAPI

\- Frontend: Next.js, TypeScript

\- Database: PostgreSQL-ready architecture

\- Monorepo: Turbo

\- AI Layer: Seller insight agents



\## Goal



SellerMate aims to become a practical seller operating system for Bangladeshi e-commerce sellers.



Not a marketplace yet.  

First goal: seller dashboard, order management, inventory, customer tracking, and daily business actions.
